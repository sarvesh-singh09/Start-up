About Care Helper

Care Helper is a unique online platform designed to help caregivers, healthcare professionals, and family members to support the well-being of elderly individuals. By offering instant access to reliable information, medication advice, and mental health support, we aim to make a difference in the lives of those who need it most.

Features

  • 24/7 Accessible Chatbot
  • Personalized Care Plans
  • Emergency Contact Integration
  • Healthcare Professionals Support
